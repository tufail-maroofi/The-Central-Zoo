Get In Touch With Us

At The Central Zoo, we are dedicated to making your visit a unique and unforgettable experience. We value your feedback and welcome any comments or ideas you may have on how we can improve. Whether you have a question about one of our animals, an upcoming event, or just want to share your thoughts, our team is here to listen. Simply fill out the form on our contact us page and a member of our team will get back to you promptly. Your input is essential to us and we take every suggestion seriously. We believe that by working together, we can make the Central Zoo an even better place for visitors, animals and staff alike. Our goal is to provide a one-of-a-kind adventure for all of our guests, and we're constantly striving to enhance the experience with new and exciting programs, events and exhibits. So don't be shy! We're eager to hear from you and make your time at The Central Zoo a memorable one. From the unique species of animals, to the educational programs and events, we're committed to providing a truly special experience for all. Let us know how we can make your visit even more amazing.
